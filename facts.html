Facts

Duke Ellington
  • Born on April 28, 1899 in Washington, D.C.
  • Died on May 24, 1974
  • Professional musician who played piano
  • Played in the Swing Era
Louis Armstrong
  • Born on August 4, 1901 in New Orleans, Louisiana
  • Died on July 6, 1971
  • Professional musician who played trumpet and was a vocalist
  • Played in the Swing Era
Count Basie
  • Born on August 21, 1904 in Red Bank, New Jersey
  • Died on April 26, 1984
  • Professional musician who played piano
  • Played in the Swing Era
Billie Holiday
  • Born on April 7, 1915 in Baltimore, Maryland
  • Died on July 17, 1959
  • Professional musician who was a vocalist
  • Played in the Swing Era
Frank Sinatra
  • Born on December 1915 in Hoboken, New Jersey
  • Died on May 14, 1998
  • Professional musician who was a vocalist
  • Played in the Swing Era
Thelonious Monk
  • Born on October 10, 1917 in Rocky Mount, North Carolina
  • Died on February 17, 1982
  • Professional musician who played piano
  • Played in the Bebop, Hard Bop, Modal and Post-Bop Eras
Dizzy Gillespie
  • Born on October 21, 1917 in Cheraw, South Carolina
  • Died on January 6, 1993
  • Professional musician who played trumpet
  • Played in the Bebop Era
Art Blakey
  • Born on October 11, 1919 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania
  • Died on October 16, 1990
  • Professional musician who played drums
  • Played in the Bebop and Hard Bop Eras
Charlie Parker
  • Born on August 29, 1920 in Kansas City, Kansas
  • Died on March 12, 1955
  • Professional musician who played alto saxophone
  • Played in the Bebop Era
Max Roach
  • Born on January 10, 1924 in New Land, North Carolina
  • Died on August 17, 2007
  • Professional musician who played drums
  • Played in the Bebop, Hard Bop, and Post-Bop Eras
J.J. Johnson
  • Born on January 22, 1924 in Indianapolis, Indiana
  • Died on February 4, 2001
  • Professional musician who played trombone
  • Played in the Bebop, Cool Jazz, and Hard Bop Eras
Miles Davis
  • Born on May 26, 1926 in Alton, Illinois
  • Died on September 28, 1991
  • Professional musician who played trumpet
  • Played in the Bebop, Cool Jazz, Hard Bop, Modal and Post-Bop Eras
John Coltrane
  • Born on September 23, 1926 in Hamlet, North Carolina
  • Died on July 17, 1967
  • Professional musician who played tenor saxophone, alto saxophone and flute
  • Played in the Free Jazz, Hard Bop, Modal and Post-Bop Eras
